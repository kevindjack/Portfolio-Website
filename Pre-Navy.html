Education

Even though Kevin got off to a rocky start with education, over the years he has worked hard to overcome this once-weakness. Here are some of his academic achievements.

Degrees

Master of Science in Joint Command, Control, and Computer Systems

Conferred on June 18, 2010. This degree was earned from Naval Postgraduate School, in Monterey, California. Kevin wrote his thesis on how an international supply chain could lead to malicious hardware being inserted into computers or other network components for a foreign entity to gain unauthorized access to computers, information, and networks within the United States Navy.

Master of Science in Strategic Intelligence

Conferred on July 25, 2019. This degree was earned from National Intelligence University, in Washington, DC. Kevin wrote his thesis on Iran's current threat against US Department of Defense computer networks and assessed whether Iran would become more or less of a threat over time.

Master of Arts in National Security and Strategic Studies

Conferred on June 19, 2015. This degree was earned from Naval War College in Newport, Rhode Island.

Bachelor of Science in Business Administration

Conferred on December 14, 2007. This degree was earned from The Citadel in Charleston, South Carolina. Kevin also earned two Minors: International Relations and Spanish.

High School Diploma

Awarded on June 9, 2000. This diploma was earned from Butte Valley Adult School in Dorris, California. Kevin had dropped out of his original high school, North Medford High School, before graduating. He completed his high school diploma to be able to join the United States Navy.

Other Education

Navy Information Professional Officer

Commissioned on December 14, 2007 and served in a variety of IT Management roles.

Enlisted Electronics Technician

Enlisted in the United States Navy as an Electronics Technician on May 4, 2000.
